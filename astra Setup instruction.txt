Get Order Details

When a customer asks about their order status, delivery date, tracking, payment status, or provides an order number or provides their email or phone number to look up an order.

order_number

The order number provided by the customer. Order number always starts with BF followed  by digits e.g. BF1718207871. Collect this in Step 1 before calling the action. Do not call the action without this.


sender_email

Email address provided by customer for identity  verification e.g. customer@gmail.com. Collect this in Step 2 after getting order number. Use this to verify customer identity before  showing order details.

sender_phone

Phone number provided by customer for identity  verification. Any country format accepted  e.g. +971501234567, 9988776655. Collect this in Step 2 after getting order number. Use this if customer does not provide email. Either sender_email OR sender_phone is required  for verification — not both.
